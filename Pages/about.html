Our Story

Ronewa’s Bakery was established in 2023 in a town called Musina. It was established by a young lady who is passionate about baking and offering her baked goods to everyone. Ronewa’s Bakery has been successful since then. What started with a passion for baking fresh bread and warm pastries has now grown into a community favorite. Since opening our doors, we’ve been committed to providing handmade, delicious baked goods that bring joy to every bite.

Our Mission

At Ronewa's Bakery, our mission is to create high-quality, freshly baked treats made with love, tradition, and the finest ingredients. We strive to be the heart of the neighborhood – a place where families gather, friends connect, and the smell of baking feels like home.

Our Team

Our team consist of Ronewa the head baker, Lethabo our assistant baker and Thabo that handles the enquires, orders and other stuuf.

Our Ingredients

We believe that great taste comes from great ingredients. That’s why we use organic flour, farm-fresh eggs, real butter, and seasonal fruits in everything we make. No shortcuts. No preservatives.
